The all-time best movies according to Alfin

Here are my three favorite movies of all time based on genre.

Comic Book:

Captain America: The Winter Soldier

Great action scenes with an engaging plot.
The movie is a great sequel to the original film.

Adventure:

Mission: Impossible - Ghost Protocol

An awesome action packed film with great stunts.
Suitable for those who don't like comic book films.

Sci-fi:

The Martian

This film has breathtaking visuals.
It's about a man's perseverance and fortitude in crisis.
